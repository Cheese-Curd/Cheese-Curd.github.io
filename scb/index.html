Sad Chill Bot, or SCB for short, is a simple joke/administrative Discord bot.

Commands:
$watermelon | Sends a picture of a watermelon.
$cbt | Co- wait I Can't Say That? Darn.
$whatiswikipedia | What is it?
$ercodes | Error Codes Duh.
$hlvr | Half - Life VR But The A.I. Is Self Aware.
$rtd | Roll The Dice.
$fac | Flip A Coin.
$killme | Fixed Bug Where You Died IRL
$die | Kill The Bot?
$kick | [RESRICTED TO BAN/KICK RIGHTS] Kicks A Member.
$ban | [RESRICTED TO BAN/KICK RIGHTS] Bans A Member.
$killem | Fixed Bug Where Everyone Died IRL
$invite | Invite the bot.
$info | Sends Server and Bot Info.
$music | Plays music ;)
$drinklava | please don't hurt her
$cheesequote | says a quote about cheese (do not use if lactose intolerant may have side effects)
$spilledmilk | haha milk go brrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrr
$scare | scares someone you ping ;3
$dm | DMs a user you ping
$whois | Get some info on someone you ping (not their IP you onion)
$musiclist | Trey's fav music
$csmusiclist | Child Safe Version of the $musiclist command
$rng [min] [max] | Ramdom Number Generator
$8ball | Magic 8ball :0
$action | Do an action ($action help for command arguments)
$showme | Shows you an item
